### 线性组合

(terminology::**Linear Combination**)

$$\begin{align}
\mathbf{v}=\begin{bmatrix}
v_{1} \\
v_{2}
\end{bmatrix}  \quad 
\mathbf{w}=\begin{bmatrix}
w_{1} \\
w_{2}
\end{bmatrix} \\
\end{align}$$

$$\begin{align}
c\mathbf{v}+d\mathbf{w}=\begin{bmatrix}
cv_{1}+dw_{1} \\
cv_{2}+dw_{2}
\end{bmatrix}
\end{align}$$

#### 1. 向量加法

**Vector Addition**
$$\begin{align}
\mathbf{v}  +\mathbf{w}=\begin{bmatrix}
v_{1}+w_{1} \\
v_{2}+w_{2}
\end{bmatrix}
\end{align}$$

#### 2. 标量数乘

**Scalar Multiplication** 
$$\begin{align}
k\mathbf{v}=\begin{bmatrix}
kv_{1} \\
kv_{2}
\end{bmatrix}
\end{align}$$

### Vector equation 
$$\begin{align}
c\mathbf{v}+d\mathbf{w}=\mathbf{b}
\end{align}$$
